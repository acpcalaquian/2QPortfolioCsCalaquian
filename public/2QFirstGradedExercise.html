𝓟ompompurin⊹ ࣪ ˖

Pompompurin is a yellow Golden Retriever, born on a sunny day on April 16th. He is known for his trademark brown beret and his laidback and friendly personality. He lives in his own basket in his owner’s home, spending most of his time relaxing, napping, hanging out with friends or occasionally exercising!

Aside from that, Pompompurin loves to eat and has a huge appetite! His most favorite foods being milk and his mama's pudding. He also enjoys collecting(and hiding) his family's shoes. His dream in life is to get bigger and bigger! His best friends are a hamster named Muffin, a mouse named Scone, and a bird named Custard.
